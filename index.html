What is Poppy's Cat Training?

You may have thought that teaching your cat to behave is next to impossible. That is far from the truth. At Poppy's Cat Training, I have been training cats for years and I've come to the realization that cats, with their more lax personalities, are typically easier to train than dogs. There are some exceptions, sure. But when it really comes down to it, cats are less rowdy and way more calm. I've trained cats since the moment that I had one, and it's my mission to help teach the world about the wonders of training cats with my cheap classes.
